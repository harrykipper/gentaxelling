; We test 4 mechanisms to establish the "highest and best use" of a residential unit defined as follows:

== :London: ==
mean of non empty patches in radius 2 OR 
mean of patches in radius 2 * 0.65
+ premium

== :New: ==
max of neighbours8 OR 
(mean of neighbourhood + premium)

== :Mean: ==
mean of neighbours8 OR mean of neighbourhood 
+ premium

== :Max: ==
max of neighbourhood OR max of neighbours8
+ premium
